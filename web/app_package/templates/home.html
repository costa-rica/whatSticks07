What Sticks (WS) is a sleep and weather tracking application. There have been studies that trace the temperature you sleep in with the quality of sleep you get. This is a step in trying to measure this in a personalized way. WS believes that we have access to too much technology and data to be taking references to broad averages. Instead, we should be personalizing health and wellness.

Once register and the account is updated with the Oura Ring Token and location information WS will collect your Oura Ring Sleep Score and the average temperature of your location.

As the data accumulates you will be able to see the correlation between your sleep score and the temperature.
